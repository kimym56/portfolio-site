Match Home/About Rhythm

Remove extra box borders, use a text-led tab switcher, and turn project items into editorial rows with dividers. This fits your current site language better.

Recommended

  • Keep the current page title and subtitle block
  • Replace the bordered segmented control with a lighter text tab treatment
  • Replace project cards with divider-based rows or much softer cards

What To Avoid

  • Stacking a bordered toggle on top of bordered cards
  • Adding another wrapper panel around the project content
  • Using borders as the main source of hierarchy
