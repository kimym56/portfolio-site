# About Copy Refresh Design

## Context

The current About page copy is professional but generic. It does not explicitly describe YongMin Kim as a developer interested in UX/HCI, and it does not reflect his newer interest in design engineering.

## Goal

Present YongMin in a clear and concise way as a developer who cares about UX/HCI and is increasingly interested in design engineering, while keeping the section easy to scan.

## Approved Copy

**Subtitle**

`A developer interested in UX/HCI, thoughtful interfaces, and design engineering.`

**Paragraphs**

1. `I'm a developer with a strong interest in UX and HCI, and I care about building products that are both useful and intuitive.`
2. `Recently, I've become especially interested in design engineering: the space between design and frontend development where thoughtful interaction, visual polish, and implementation quality come together.`
3. `I enjoy creating interfaces that are clear, usable, and built with long-term maintainability in mind.`

## Notes

Keep the existing page structure unchanged. If the About section grows later, the highest-value additions would be collaboration style, accessibility priorities, prototyping interests, and the kinds of product problems YongMin most enjoys solving.
